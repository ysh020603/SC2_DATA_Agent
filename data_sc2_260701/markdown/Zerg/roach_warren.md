## Description

The Roach Warren is a building that gives the Zerg player the ability to create and upgrade Roaches . The two upgrades that are available at the Roach Warren are Tunneling Claws , and Glial Reconstitution .

Roaches are common in most matchups, so this building is often added after the pool for early defence and larvae efficiency.

## Upgrades

### Glial Reconstitution

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 79 Hotkey G Researched from: Roach Warren Requires: Lair Increases Roach movement speed to 4.2 (+1.05) while unburrowed, and to 4.4 while burrowed under creep.

### Tunneling Claws

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 79 Hotkey T Researched from: Roach Warren Requires: Lair Gives Roaches the ability to move while burrowed at a speed of 2.8.
