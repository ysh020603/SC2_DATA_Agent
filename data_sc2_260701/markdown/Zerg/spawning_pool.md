## Description

The Spawning Pool is the first Zerg tech building; it unlocks Zerg's basic fighting units - Zerglings . Building the Spawning pool also unlocks the Lair , Roach Warren , Baneling Nest , Spine Crawler , Spore Crawler and the Queen .

Two upgrades are available at the Spawning Pool, Adrenal Glands , and Metabolic Boost .

## Upgrades

### Metabolic Boost

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 79 Hotkey M Researched from: Spawning Pool Increases Zergling movement speed by 60% to 6.58 (+2.45).

### Adrenal Glands

Minerals 200 Gas 200 Game Speed 93 Hotkey A Researched from: Spawning Pool Decreases Zergling attack cooldown to 0.35 (-0.15). Requires a Hive .
