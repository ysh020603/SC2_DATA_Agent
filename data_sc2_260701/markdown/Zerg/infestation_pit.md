## Description

The Infestation Pit is a second-tier Zerg building that unlocks the Hive , Infestor , and Swarm Host , as well as the Infestor upgrades. The player may upgrade Neural Parasite at the Infestation Pit.

## Upgrades

Minerals 150 Gas 150 Game Speed 79 Hotkey E Researched from: Infestation Pit Allows Infestors to use the Neural Parasite ability.
