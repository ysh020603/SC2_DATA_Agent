## Description

Requiring a Lair to build, this building contains access to all Flyer units and Upgrades listed below. The Spire is needed for the construction of the Zerg aerial units Mutalisk , a fast harasser and the Corruptor , an anti-air unit.

Once a Hive has finished, the Spire may be further upgraded to a Greater Spire for Broodlord end-game. Flyer Carapace affects all Spire units, which all have a large health bar and cost, and will give the most effect right away but Broodlords also need Evolution Chamber 's Ground Attack and Carapace upgrades for Broodlings and Ultralisk -tech switches to be more effective.

### Mutate into Greater Spire

Minerals 100 Gas 150 Game Speed 71 Hotkey G Researched from: Spire Contains upgrades for Zerg air units. | Enables Brood Lords from Corruptors .

Important to note is the Spire and Greater Spire 's long building time, making this a high level target for enemy drops, it is recommended to have two Spires and protect both from Drops and Run Bys from the enemy army.
