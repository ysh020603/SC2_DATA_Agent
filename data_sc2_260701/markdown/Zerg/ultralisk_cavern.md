## Description

The Ultralisk Cavern is one of two Zerg tech buildings that are available after the completion of Hive . After the Ultralisk Cavern is completed, Ultralisks can be built and their Chitinous Plating and Anabolic Synthesis can be upgraded in it.

## Upgrades

### Chitinous Plating

Minerals 150 Gas 150 Game Speed 79 Hotkey C Researched from: Ultralisk Cavern This upgrade increases the armor of all Ultralisks by 2.

### Anabolic Synthesis

Minerals 150 Gas 150 Game Speed 42.9 Hotkey A Researched from: Ultralisk Cavern Increases the movement speed of Ultralisks off creep.
