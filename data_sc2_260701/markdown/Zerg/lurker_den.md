## Overview

The Lurker Den was developed in 2010, but cut in the alpha stages of StarCraft II , and has since been brought back with Legacy of the Void . The Lurker Den can be morphed from a Drone once a Hydralisk Den has been built. Prior to patch 4.0, the Lurker Den was morphed from the Hydralisk Den . The Lurker Den allows Lurkers to be morphed from Hydralisks .

## Lurker Upgrades

### Adaptive Talons

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 57 Hotkey D Researched from: Lurker Den Requires: Hive Reduces the time Lurkers take to burrow from 2 seconds to 1.07 seconds.

### Seismic Spines

Minerals 150 Gas 150 Game Speed 57 Hotkey S Researched from: Lurker Den Requires: Hive Increases the range of the Lurkers attack from 8 to 10.
