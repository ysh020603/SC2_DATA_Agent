## Description

The Forge is a structure in the Protoss Tech Tree , which requires a Nexus before it can be warped in. The Forge is the research center for Ground Weapons , Ground Armor , and Shields upgrades. It also unlocks the Photon Cannon .

Like most Protoss structures, the Forge can only be warped in at a location powered by a Pylon . It can only research upgrades while it remains within the range of the Pylon , otherwise it becomes unpowered and cannot function. An unpowered Forge still allows the creation of Photon Cannons .

## Ground Weapons Upgrades

These upgrades affect the following units: Archon , Colossus , Dark Templar , Sentry , Immortal , Stalker , Zealot . A Twilight Council is needed in order to research Levels 2 and 3.

### Ground Weapons Level 1

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 121 Hotkey G Researched from: Forge Upgrades the damage dealt by Protoss ground units.

This upgrade is important in PvZ match as it allows Zealots to kill Zerglings in 2 hits instead of 3.

### Ground Weapons Level 2

Minerals 150 Gas 150 Game Speed 145 Hotkey G Researched from: Forge Further upgrades the damage dealt by Protoss ground units. Requires a Twilight Council .

### Ground Weapons Level 3

Minerals 200 Gas 200 Game Speed 168 Hotkey G Researched from: Forge Maximizes the damage dealt by Protoss ground units. Requires a Twilight Council .

This upgrade will allow a Colossus to kill Hydralisks in two shots.

## Competitive Use

Because it unlocks the Photon Cannon, the Forge is a central part of Cannon Rushes . If a player builds a Forge before a Gateway , that is often a sign that a Cannon Rush is underway.

In PvZ, Forge Fast Expand is a common opening and involves a Forge being constructed as the first part of a wall blocking access to the natural in order to unlock access to the Photon Cannon for defensive purposes.
