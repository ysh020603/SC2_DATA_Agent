## Description

The Tech Lab is an add-on for the Barracks , Factory and Starport that unlocks new tech and upgrades. For example, a Barracks with a Tech Lab can produce Marauder and Ghost units.

Players can exchange the building attached to a Tech Lab to achieve tech swaps, tech rushes or early unit production. For instance, when doing a fast Banshee build, players can simultaneously build a Tech Lab on a Factory while also building the Starport. When the Tech Lab finishes, the player can float the Factory away and land the Starport next to the now free Tech Lab. Now the player can build Banshees immediately, without having to wait for a Tech Lab to build.

## Effect on Buildings

### Barracks

Unlocks Marauders and is a requirement for Ghosts .

### Combat Shield

Shield | Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 79 Hotkey C Researched from: Tech Lab Every Marine gains a Combat Shield that gives 10 hitpoints.

### Stimpack

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 100 Hotkey T Researched from: Tech Lab Every Marine and Marauder gain the Stimpack ability.

### Concussive Shells

Minerals 50 Gas 50 Game Speed 43 Hotkey G Researched from: Tech Lab Marauders gain the Concussive Shells ability.

### Factory

Unlocks Siege Tanks and is a requirement for Thors .

### Infernal Pre-Igniter

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 79 Hotkey I Researched from: Tech Lab Improves the Hellion 's bonus against Light units by +5 damage and the Hellbat 's bonus against Light units by +12 damage.

### Mag-Field Accelerator

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 100 Hotkey R Researched from: Tech Lab Increases Cyclone Lock On damage. Deals 600 damage over 14 seconds to ground and flying targets.

### Drilling Claws

Minerals 75 Gas 75 Game Speed 79 Hotkey C Researched from: Tech Lab Requires: Armory Allows Widow Mines to burrow, unborrow and activate two times as fast. Additionally this grants invisibility while reloading.

### Smart Servos

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 79 Hotkey S Researched from: Tech Lab Requires: Armory Allows Hellions , Hellbats , Vikings , and Thors to transform quickly between combat modes.

### Starport

Unlocks Ravens and Banshees and is a requirement for Battlecruisers .

### Cloaking Field

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 79 Hotkey C Researched from: Tech Lab Allows Banshees to use the Cloak ability.

### Hyperflight Rotors

Minerals 125 Gas 125 Game Speed 79 s Hotkey B Researched from: Tech Lab Increases the movement speed of Banshees from 3.85 to 5.25.

### Interference Matrix

Minerals 50 Gas 50 Game Speed 57 Hotkey E Researched from: Tech Lab Allows Ravens to use the Interference Matrix ability.

## Other

- While there is ongoing research in a Tech Lab, the attached building cannot lift off.
- When a Tech Lab is destroyed, units that have started production in that building will finish regardless of now-missing tech requirements. Queued units which require the Tech Lab will be canceled.

## Historical and Patch Information

### Removed Upgrades

#### Enhanced Munitions

Minerals 150 Gas 150 Game Speed 79 Hotkey E Researched from: Tech Lab Increases the Raven's Anti-Armor Missile explosion radius by 20% and its tracking range by 50%.

#### Mag-Field Launchers

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 79 Hotkey R Researched from: Tech Lab Increases Cyclone 's attack range by +2.

#### Recalibrated Explosives

Minerals 150 Gas 150 Game Speed 79 Hotkey E Researched from: Tech Lab Increases the Seeker Missile unit's tracking range by 50% (13 to 19.5) and Increases the Seeker Missile's damage by 30%.

#### Rapid Fire Launchers

Minerals 75 Gas 75 Game Speed 79 Hotkey A Researched from: Tech Lab Increases the attack speed of the first 12 shots of the Cyclone's Lock On.

#### Corvid Reactor

Minerals 150 Gas 150 Game Speed 79 Hotkey T Researched from: Tech Lab Increases the Raven 's starting energy by 25.

#### Hurricane Engines

Minerals 100 Gas 100 Game Speed 100 Hotkey R Researched from: Tech Lab Increases the move speed of Cyclone 's to 4.73.

Mag-Field Accelerator was removed in Patch 5.0.12 and put back in Patch 5.0.14.
