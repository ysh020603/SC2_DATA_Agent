## Description

The Cyclone is a fast hit-and-away vehicle produced from a Factory with an attached Tech Lab . It was first introduced in Legacy of the Void .

The Cyclone has an ability called Lock On which does 20 damage (with or without weapon upgrades) and ignores armor, and has a longer range and allows it to shoot while moving. Paired with a high movement speed, this makes the Cyclone a master at kiting .

## Abilities

### Lock On

Caster: Cyclone Range 7 (15) Cooldown 4.29 s Duration 14.28 s Hotkey C Locks the Cyclone's weapons on the target unit, increasing the Cyclone's range to 15 and allowing it to move while firing. Cancels if target moves out of range.

To use Lock On , a Cyclone must move within 7 range of a target. Once the ability is activated, a Cyclone can move away from the target up to a distance of 15 while still maintaining effect. Lock On lasts for 14.28 seconds after activation, but the effect is broken if the target dies, moves out of vision or exceeds the maximum distance of 15. It cannot be cancelled early by any other means.

By default, Lock On is set to auto-cast. Lock On Auto-Cast range is 7.5 for Ground units and 7 for Air units. Auto-cast will only activate when the Cyclone is not on a move command, and it will only target units (with the exception of Larvae and Broodlings ) and combat buildings. When multiple eligible targets are in range, closer targets are prioritized.

## Competitive Use

|  | This section may be outdated due to metagame or patch changes. Help us by revising it . \| This article was last edited by DefianceSC2 on 25-Apr-2026 17:46 (UTC+0) . |
|---|---|

- Since patch 5.0.14 Cyclones are again only buildable from a factory with a Tech Lab . Because of their long range and anti-mech bonus, Cyclones are a suitable option against early harassing units such as Medivacs , Banshees , Oracles or Warp Prisms .
- Cyclones lose their mobility advantage against fast mass-produced units and upgrades like Stimpacked and Concussive Shells Marines / Marauders , Zerglings with Metabolic Boost , Blink Stalkers or Zealots with Charge .
- Lock On has a casting range of 7. Once Locked on the Cyclones range increases to 15 making it difficult for slow tanky units to escape.
- The Cyclone's default weapon has the same DPS. As such the only use for the Cyclone's default attack over Lock On is for unusual target prioritization situations.
- The fast movement speed of the Cyclone allows for an unorthodox and fragile mechanical style composition that excels at pressuring the opponent and sniping.

### Vs. Protoss

- A counter to early-game Stargate units and Gateway units. Outrange Adepts and Oracles as well as dealing considerable damage to Stalkers since they are mech units.
- Immortals are a hard counter to them.
- Can be effectively used early in the game to pressure a greedy Protoss and snipe key units before they become counters such as Stalkers before Blink and Zealots without Charge . Phoenixes are less effective verse Cyclones as the new Lock On ability does not have a cooldown. To a more micro-intensive (and risky) extent, Warp Prisms loaded with Immortals can effectively counter Cyclones with pickup/drop play.

### Vs. Terran

- Against Banshee openings, a single Cyclone is capable of dealing with a single Banshee if it is micro'd away from the Banshee and maintains detection/vision, do note that the Cyclone is faster and Lock On gives it 15 effective range.
- Against early Marines + Siege Tank , pushes common in this mirror match, Cyclones do little except spot the attack coming across the map. Mass Hellions to kill the Marines (leaving the Tanks defenseless) is far more useful. The Raven 's Interference Matrix forcibly cancels Lock On.

### Vs. Zerg

- Faster than Roaches and Ravagers off creep, but outrun by Zerglings . Slower than Roaches or Ravagers on creep. Banshees are more often made blindly to counter Roaches and force Spore Crawlers .

- The Hellion / Cyclone style is common against Zerg as the Hellion can cover the Cyclones during Lock On from their counter: Zerglings allowing for effective poke damage and/or sniping bases. Burrowed units (especially Banelings ) are highly effective against this extremely mobile force and/or Infestors with Fungal Growth . Vipers can also use Abduct to separate the more dangerous Cyclones from the main army. Beware to not lose the Viper to a Lock On however.

- Used to protect Battlecruisers from being sniped by Corruptors .

## Historical and Patch Information

### Description

For 2 years, from Patch 3.8.0 (Nov 2016) until Patch 4.7.1 (Nov 2018), the Cyclone filled a vastly different role: it did not require a Tech Lab, was not designed for kiting but rather raw damage, and was a little sturdier, too. As a result it was the centerpiece of many an early-game push. The Lock On ability was restricted to air targets for a period.

### Abilities

On Patch 5.0.14, the Lock On cooldown was 2.86 (≈ 4 / 1.4) seconds at Faster speed instead of 4.29 (≈ 6 / 1.4) seconds. [ 1 ] However, this was corrected in patch 5.0.15.

### Upgrades

#### Removed Upgrades
